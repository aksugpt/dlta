Lubricant Labels

Lubricant labels are distinctive from other labels as it is applied on greasy and slippery products; it has different compositions, which require high abrasive strength and water-proof material that we are committed to provide in our labels. They are applied on the containers of diesel oil, hair oil, Mobil oil, etc. Our clients have appreciated and reckoned quality of our labels that holds vigorously to the product for extensive period, as we never compromise on the superiority of raw material and printing technology used.
